# Comprehensive UI/UX Audit - SleekInvoices

**Date:** January 4, 2026
**Goal:** Identify all missing elements, errors, and incomplete features to achieve best-in-class experience

---

## Audit Methodology

1. **Heuristic Evaluation** - Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics
2. **Competitive Analysis** - Compare with FreshBooks, Wave, Invoice Ninja
3. **User Flow Testing** - Test all critical paths end-to-end
4. **Visual Design Review** - Consistency, hierarchy, accessibility
5. **Technical Review** - Performance, errors, edge cases
