## Part 1: Current Feature Inventory

### Dashboard Features ✅
- Total Revenue display
- Outstanding balance tracking
- Total invoice count
- Paid invoice count
- Recent invoices list (5 most recent)
- Quick "New Invoice" button

### Invoice Management ✅
- Create invoices with line items
- Edit invoices
- Delete invoices
- Send invoices via email
- Generate PDF invoices
- Duplicate invoices
- Invoice status tracking (Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue, Canceled)
- Invoice numbering with custom prefix
- Payment status badges (Unpaid, Partial, Paid)
- Payment filters
- Search by invoice number or client name
- Status filters

### Client Management ✅
- Add clients
- Edit client information
- Delete clients
- Client contact details (name, email, phone, address)
- Tax ID tracking
- Client notes

### Payment Tracking ✅
- Record manual payments (cash, check, bank transfer, other)
- Stripe payment link generation
- Payment history per invoice
- Automatic invoice status updates on payment
- Payment reconciliation

### Advanced Features ✅
- Multi-currency support (9 currencies)
- Exchange rate management
- Automated recurring invoices (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Client portal with secure token access
- Automated overdue detection (daily cron)
- Discount support (percentage/fixed)
- Tax calculation
- Notes and payment terms

### Analytics ✅
- Basic revenue statistics
- Invoice status breakdown
- Payment status tracking
