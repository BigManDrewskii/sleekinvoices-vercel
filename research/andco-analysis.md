# AND.CO Competitor Analysis

## Overview
AND.CO (now Fiverr Workspace) is an invoicing tool specifically targeting freelancers who sell services. Acquired by Fiverr in 2018.

## Key Features
- Flexible invoice scheduling
- Contracts and proposals creation
- Time tracking with timer
- Project management
- Bank transaction import
- Mobile apps (iOS/Android)

## Pricing
- Free: 1 client only, AND.CO branding on invoices
- Pro: $18/month (annual) or $24/month (monthly)
- Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Stripe/PayPal)

## Strengths (Pros)
- Easy to set up and use
- Flexible invoice scheduling
- Creates detailed contracts and proposals
- Home pages for projects and clients
- Good mobile apps
- Step-by-step invoice wizard

## Weaknesses (Cons)
- Only ONE invoice template
- Limited customization of invoices
- No product/service records with prices
- Must remember prices (no master price list)
- Free version limited to 1 client

## Target Audience
- Freelancers who sell services
- Independent contractors
- Small creative businesses

## SleekInvoices Differentiators
Based on this analysis, SleekInvoices can differentiate by:

1. **Multiple Templates** - We offer 6+ customizable templates vs AND.CO's single template
2. **Better Customization** - Full color, font, and layout customization
3. **QuickBooks Integration** - Two-way sync with QuickBooks (AND.CO doesn't have this)
4. **Crypto Payments** - Support for cryptocurrency payments
5. **AI-Powered Features** - Magic Invoice with AI assistance
6. **More Generous Free Tier** - 50 invoices/month vs 1 client limit
7. **Professional PDF Generation** - High-quality PDF output

## Landing Page Messaging Insights
- Focus on "professional" and "customizable" (AND.CO weakness)
- Highlight template variety
- Emphasize QuickBooks integration
- Position as "more than just freelancer invoicing"
- Clean, modern aesthetic (AND.CO looks dated)
